🕌 FSSS COMMUNITY CENTRAL MOSQUE

Vision

Is to be a leading Masjid that builds a strong faith based on Allah-consciousness, piety, and mindfulness of Allah, serving as a spiritual shield against sins and wrongdoings.

Mission

Is to provide deep knowledge of the Quran and Sunnah of the Prophet (PBOH) through teaching and learning, provide a safe, holy environment for worship while highlighting the true values of Islam through regular, modern, and sustainable quality services for the care of the affairs of Muslims in our community.

Core Value

To live a life of Quran and Sunnah as practised by the Prophet (PBOH) and exemplified by the Sahaba.

Organisation Structure of Masjid

The organisation Structure of the Masjid is designed for smooth administration without undermining the authority of Imam as the supreme authority according to the Sunnah of the Prophet (PBOH). The organisation Structure is divided into:

  • Spiritual Structure: Headed by Imam
  • Administrative Structure: Headed by Amir

For the effectiveness and efficiency of executive decisions, various committees and subcommittees assist in executing programmes and projects of the Masjid.

📚 Programs /البرامج

Our mosque offers a variety of programs to enrich spiritual, educational, and social life.
.تقدم مسجدنا مجموعة متنوعة من البرامج لتعزيز الحياة الروحية والتعليمية والاجتماعية

📖

Qur’an Memorization / حفظ القرآن

Structured classes for children, youth, and adults to memorize and understand the Quran.
دروس منظمة للأطفال والشباب والكبار لحفظ وفهم القرآن

🎤

Islamic Lectures / المحاضرات الإسلامية

Weekly lectures covering theology, jurisprudence, and contemporary issues.
محاضرات أسبوعية تغطي العقيدة، الفقه، والقضايا المعاصرة

🌱

Youth Development / تنمية الشباب

Programs developing leadership, ethics, and community service among youth.
برامج تهدف لتطوير القيادة والأخلاق وخدمة المجتمع بين الشباب

👩‍🏫

Children-Focused Programs / برامج خاصة بالاولاد

Educational, spiritual, and social activities for children in the community.
أنشطة تعليمية وروحية واجتماعية للاولاد في المجتمع

🤝 Community Projects | مشاريع المجتمع

Our initiatives aim to uplift communities through education, health, welfare, and spiritual growth.
تهدف مبادراتنا إلى رفع مستوى المجتمعات من خلال التعليم والصحة والرعاية والنمو الروحي

Social Welfare & Humanitarian Aid
الرعاية الاجتماعية والمساعدات الإنسانية

Providing support for families, youth, and the needy through essential aid and social programs.
تقديم الدعم للأسر والشباب والمحتاجين من خلال المساعدات الأساسية والبرامج الاجتماعية

Education Support
دعم التعليم

Offering learning materials, scholarships, and mentorship programs to empower students.
تقديم المواد التعليمية والمنح الدراسية وبرامج الإرشاد لتمكين الطلاب

Health Outreach
الخدمات الصحية

Delivering medical care, health awareness campaigns, and welfare assistance to communities.
تقديم الرعاية الطبية وحملات التوعية الصحية والمساعدات المجتمعية

Zakat & Sadaqah Distribution
توزيع الزكاة والصدقات

Ensuring transparent and fair support for the less privileged in society.
ضمان تقديم الدعم الشفاف والعادل للأقل حظًا في المجتمع

Funeral Services
خدمات الجنائز

Providing support for funeral arrangements, guidance, and rituals according to Islamic tradition.
تقديم الدعم لترتيبات الجنائز والإرشاد والطقوس وفقاً للتقاليد الإسلامية

Da‘wah & Public Enlightenment
الدعوة والتثقيف العام

Promoting Islamic awareness through outreach, lectures, and community engagement.
تعزيز الوعي الإسلامي من خلال برامج التوعية والمحاضرات والمشاركة المجتمعية
