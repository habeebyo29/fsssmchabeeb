About FSSMC Central Mosque

FSSMC Central Mosque is a center for worship, learning, and community empowerment. We strive to nurture faith, advance knowledge, and serve the Ummah with excellence.

Mission

To guide individuals spiritually, provide comprehensive Islamic education, and serve the community with integrity, compassion, and excellence.

Vision

To be a leading hub of spiritual growth, community service, and educational excellence, inspiring a society grounded in knowledge, ethics, and faith.

Core Values

  • Spiritual Enrichment: Foster a lifelong connection to the Quran and Sunnah through inclusive education, regular worship, and mentorship.
  • Community Development: Mobilize members for social welfare projects like healthcare clinics, schools, and vocational training to empower the less fortunate.
  • Education Advancement: Promote Islamic scholarship by organizing seminars, publishing research, and supporting students in religious and secular studies.
  • Interfaith Harmony: Build bridges of understanding with other faith communities through dialogue, collaboration on humanitarian initiatives, and outreach.
  • Youth Engagement: Cultivate future Muslim leaders through leadership training, youth camps, and civic participation opportunities.
