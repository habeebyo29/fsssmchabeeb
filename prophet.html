Prayer Guide (Salah)

Learn how to perform the five daily prayers step by step, including ablution (Wudu) and correct postures for each part of Salah.

💧

Step 1: Wudu (Ablution)

Perform ablution to purify yourself: wash hands, mouth, nose, face, arms, wipe head, and feet as taught by Prophet Muhammad ﷺ.

🕋

Step 2: Niyyah (Intention)

Face the Qiblah (direction of Makkah) and make the intention in your heart for the specific prayer you are about to perform.

🙏

Step 3: Takbir & Qiyam

Raise your hands and say “Allahu Akbar” to start the prayer, then recite Surah Al-Fatihah and another portion of the Qur’an while standing.

🙇‍♂️

Step 4: Ruku & Sujood

Bow (Ruku) with hands on knees, then prostrate (Sujood) on the ground with forehead, nose, palms, knees, and toes touching. Repeat as required.

🕌

Step 5: Tashahhud & Taslim

While sitting, recite the Tashahhud and then conclude the prayer by turning your head to the right and left saying “Assalamu Alaikum wa Rahmatullah”.
