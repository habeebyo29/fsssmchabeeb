🌙 Last 10 Nights

The last ten nights of Ramadan hold special blessings. Intensify your worship, prayer, and charity during these nights, especially on the odd nights for Laylatul Qadr.

  • Nightly Qiyam al-Layl: 1:00 AM – 4:30 AM
  • Itikaf: Spiritual retreat in the mosque for focused worship
  • Community Iftar and Charity initiatives
  • Spiritual guidance and reminders for increased worship
  • Hadith: “Seek Laylatul Qadr in the odd nights of the last ten days of Ramadan.” — Sahih al-Bukhari 2017

💡 Ramadan Tips

  • Perform extra Nafl prayers and seek forgiveness
  • Give sadaqah regularly to support community members
  • Recite and reflect on Qur’an every day
  • Maintain consistency in Dhikr and daily supplications
