Islam & Its Five Pillars

Islam is built upon five foundational pillars that guide the spiritual and practical life of every Muslim.

  • 🖼 Islam
  • 🙏 Prayer Guide (Salah)
  • 📖 Qur'an
  • 📿 Daily Azkaar
🕋

Shahada (Faith)

Declaration of faith: “There is no god but Allah, and Muhammad is His messenger.” This pillar affirms the central belief in one God and the prophethood of Muhammad ﷺ.

🙏

Salah (Prayer)

Performing the five daily prayers at prescribed times each day. Salah strengthens connection with Allah and provides spiritual discipline.

💰

Zakat (Charity)

Giving a portion of wealth to the needy and less fortunate. Zakat purifies wealth and promotes social equality in the community.

🌙

Sawm (Fasting)

Fasting during the month of Ramadan as a form of self-purification, empathy for the poor, and devotion to Allah.

🕋

Hajj (Pilgrimage)

Making the pilgrimage to Makkah at least once in a lifetime if able. Hajj unites Muslims globally and symbolizes equality before Allah.
